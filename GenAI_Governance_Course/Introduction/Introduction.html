GenAI Governance: Policies, Risk Management, and Compliance

Welcome to GenAI Governance: Policies, Risk Management, and Compliance. This comprehensive course equips you with the knowledge and frameworks to implement effective AI governance in your organization.

Why This Course Matters

Generative AI introduces unprecedented opportunities and risks. Organizations deploying AI without governance face data breaches, regulatory violations, bias incidents, and reputational damage. This course provides practical frameworks to deploy AI responsibly while maintaining innovation velocity.

Learning Objectives

By completing this course, you will be able to:

  • Understand major AI governance frameworks (NIST AI RMF, EU AI Act, ISO 42001)
  • Develop comprehensive AI policies including acceptable use and data handling
  • Define governance roles and responsibilities using RACI models
  • Conduct AI risk assessments and classify systems by risk tier
  • Implement technical controls for input validation, output filtering, and monitoring
  • Create model cards and maintain comprehensive AI documentation
  • Execute AI audits covering bias, security, and compliance
  • Navigate regulatory requirements (GDPR, EU AI Act, sector-specific regulations)
  • Respond to AI incidents and drive continuous improvement

Course Structure

This course contains 3 comprehensive modules with 9 in-depth content pages, followed by assessments to validate your understanding.

Module 1: Governance Foundations

  • AI Governance Frameworks and Standards: NIST AI RMF, EU AI Act, ISO 42001, governance structures
  • AI Policies and Acceptable Use: Policy framework, acceptable use policy, data handling, deployment standards
  • Governance Roles and Responsibilities: RACI matrix, executive roles, operational roles, cross-functional collaboration

Module 2: Risk Management and Implementation

  • AI Risk Assessment and Classification: Risk categories, assessment questionnaire, risk tiers, risk register
  • Technical Controls and Safeguards: Defense-in-depth, input validation, PII detection, output filtering, monitoring
  • Model Cards and Documentation: Model card framework, data sheets, living documentation, compliance evidence

Module 3: Compliance and Continuous Improvement

  • AI Audit Processes and Methodologies: Audit framework, bias audits, security testing, findings classification
  • Regulatory Compliance and Legal Requirements: EU AI Act, GDPR, sector regulations, compliance mapping, DPAs
  • Incident Response and Continuous Improvement: Incident types, response workflow, post-incident reviews, maturity assessment

Pros and Cons Analysis

Comprehensive analysis of AI governance advantages (risk mitigation, compliance, trust) and challenges (complexity, resources, velocity).

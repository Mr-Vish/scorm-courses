### Module 1: Governance Foundations (3 pages + assessment)
1. **AI Governance Frameworks and Standards**
   - NIST AI RMF, EU AI Act, ISO 42001, OECD, IEEE standards
   - Governance structures and maturity levels
   - Core framework comparison

2. **AI Policies and Acceptable Use**
   - Policy framework (6 policy types)
   - Acceptable use policy with tier-based approvals
   - Data handling requirements and classifications
   - Model development and deployment standards
   - Incident response policy

3. **Governance Roles and Responsibilities**
   - RACI matrix for governance activities
   - Executive roles (CAIO, AI Board)
   - Operational roles (Ethics Lead, Risk Manager, Model Owner, Data Steward)
   - Cross-functional collaboration
   - Training and competency requirements

**Assessment 1:** 10 unique questions covering frameworks, policies, and roles

### Module 2: Risk Management and Implementation (3 pages + assessment)
4. **AI Risk Assessment and Classification**
   - 6 AI-specific risk categories
   - Risk assessment questionnaire
   - 4-tier risk classification system
   - Risk register template and management
   - Continuous risk monitoring

5. **Technical Controls and Safeguards**
   - Defense-in-depth architecture (4 layers)
   - Input validation (PII detection, prompt injection prevention)
   - Model-level safeguards (system prompts, guardrails)
   - Output filtering and validation
   - Comprehensive monitoring and logging
   - Automated alerting

6. **Model Cards and Documentation**
   - Model card framework (7 sections)
   - Complete model card template
   - Data sheets for datasets
   - Documentation hierarchy for different stakeholders
   - Living documentation and version control

**Assessment 2:** 10 unique questions covering risk assessment, controls, and documentation

### Module 3: Compliance and Continuous Improvement (3 pages + assessment)
7. **AI Audit Processes and Methodologies**
   - 5 audit types and frequencies
   - 6-phase audit framework
   - Comprehensive audit checklist
   - Bias audit methodology with fairness metrics
   - Security audit (OWASP Top 10 for LLM)
   - Audit findings classification (P0-P3)

8. **Regulatory Compliance and Legal Requirements**
   - Global regulatory landscape (EU AI Act, GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, etc.)
   - EU AI Act deep dive with risk tiers and compliance roadmap
   - GDPR Article 22 (automated decision-making)
   - Sector-specific regulations (healthcare, financial services)
   - Compliance mapping process
   - Data Processing Agreements (DPAs)

9. **Incident Response and Continuous Improvement**
   - 6 AI incident types
   - Incident severity classification (P0-P3)
   - 7-step incident response workflow
   - Incident Response Team roles
   - Post-incident review template
   - Continuous improvement framework
   - Governance maturity assessment scorecard

**Assessment 3:** 10 unique questions covering audits, compliance, and incident response

- **5 Key Challenges:** Implementation complexity, resource requirements, velocity vs. control tension, evolving regulations, measurement challenges

- Best practices for effective governance

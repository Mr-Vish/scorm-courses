Advanced Prompting Patterns and Strategies

Welcome to Advanced Prompting Patterns and Strategies. Prompt engineering is more than just writing clear instructions; it's about using structured patterns to unlock the full reasoning and creative potential of Large Language Models. This course covers the most important patterns used by professional AI engineers today.

  • Reasoning and Problem-Solving Patterns
    • Chain of Thought and Reasoning (CoT)
    • Tree of Thoughts (ToT): Exploring Multiple Paths
    • Self-Reflection: Improving Through Critique
    • ReAct (Reason + Act): Interleaving Thoughts and Actions
  • Data and Information Patterns
    • Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) as a Pattern
    • Chain of Density: Mastering Semantic Compression
    • Recursive Summarization for Massive Datasets
    • Skeleton-of-Thought for High-Speed Generation

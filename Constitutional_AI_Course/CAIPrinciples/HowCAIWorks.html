How Constitutional AI Works

What is Constitutional AI (CAI)?

Constitutional AI is Anthropic's approach to training AI systems to be helpful, harmless, and honest. Instead of relying solely on human feedback (RLHF), CAI uses a set of principles - a "constitution" - to guide the model's self-improvement.

The CAI Process

  1. Red teaming: Generate harmful prompts to test the model
  2. Initial response: The model generates an unconstrained response
  3. Critique: The model critiques its own response based on constitutional principles
  4. Revision: The model revises its response to address the critique
  5. RLAIF: Use AI feedback (not human) to train a preference model
  6. RL training: Fine-tune the model using the AI preference model

Sample Constitutional Principles

  • "Please choose the response that is most helpful, while being safe and avoiding harmful content"
  • "Choose the response that is least likely to be used to cause harm"
  • "Choose the response that best demonstrates understanding of ethics"
  • "Choose the response that is most respectful of everyone's rights and dignity"

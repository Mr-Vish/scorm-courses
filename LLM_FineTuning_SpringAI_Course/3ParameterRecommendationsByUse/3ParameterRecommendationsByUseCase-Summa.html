3. Pros, Cons, and Use Cases - Summary

In this module, we compared fine-tuning with prompt engineering and RAG, highlighting that fine-tuning is best for style adaptation and latency reduction, while RAG is better for incorporating real-time information. We also discussed the importance of data quality, the risk of catastrophic forgetting, and the need for ethical auditing of training datasets.

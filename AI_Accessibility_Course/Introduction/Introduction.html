GenAI for Accessibility - Alt Text, Transcription, and Assistive Tools

Course Overview

Welcome to GenAI for Accessibility. This comprehensive course explores how Generative AI technologies are revolutionizing digital accessibility, making content accessible to over 1.3 billion people worldwide with disabilities. You will learn practical techniques for implementing AI-powered accessibility solutions including automated alt text generation, video transcription, document remediation, and compliance testing.

Digital accessibility is both a legal requirement and a moral imperative. This course equips you with the knowledge and skills to leverage AI tools effectively while maintaining the human oversight necessary for quality and compliance.

Target Audience

This course is designed for:

  • Content Creators: Writers, designers, and multimedia producers who create digital content
  • Web Developers: Frontend and full-stack developers implementing accessible web applications
  • Product Managers: Leaders responsible for accessibility strategy and compliance
  • QA Engineers: Testers validating accessibility requirements
  • Accessibility Specialists: Professionals seeking to enhance their toolkit with AI solutions
  • Business Stakeholders: Decision-makers evaluating accessibility investments

Prerequisites

To get the most from this course, you should have:

  • Basic understanding of web technologies: Familiarity with HTML, CSS, and how websites work
  • Awareness of accessibility concepts: Basic knowledge of why accessibility matters (covered in Module 1)
  • No programming experience required: Code examples are provided for reference but not mandatory to understand
  • Curiosity about AI: Interest in how AI can solve real-world problems

Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

  • Understand accessibility fundamentals: Explain WCAG 2.1 principles and legal requirements
  • Generate AI-powered alt text: Use vision-language models to create descriptive image alternatives
  • Automate video transcription: Implement AI transcription and captioning workflows
  • Remediate documents: Apply AI tools to fix accessibility issues in PDFs and documents
  • Implement web accessibility: Use ARIA and semantic HTML with AI assistance
  • Simplify content: Leverage AI for cognitive accessibility improvements
  • Build accessibility pipelines: Integrate AI tools into development workflows
  • Test and validate: Combine automated, manual, and user testing approaches
  • Ensure compliance: Meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards and legal requirements

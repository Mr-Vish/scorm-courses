GenAI Application Testing Strategies

Welcome to GenAI Application Testing Strategies. This comprehensive course equips you with the essential skills to test, evaluate, and ensure quality in GenAI applications throughout the development lifecycle.

Why GenAI Testing Is Critical

GenAI applications are fundamentally different from traditional software. They are non-deterministic, expensive to run, and can produce harmful outputs. Without proper testing strategies, you risk:

  • Deploying applications that hallucinate or provide incorrect information
  • Failing to detect prompt injection and jailbreak vulnerabilities
  • Releasing features with poor user experience due to quality issues
  • Incurring unexpected costs from inefficient implementations
  • Violating safety and compliance requirements

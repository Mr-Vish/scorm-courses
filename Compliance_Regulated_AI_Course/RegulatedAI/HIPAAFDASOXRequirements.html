HIPAA, FDA, and SOX Requirements for AI

AI in Regulated Industries

Healthcare, finance, and legal sectors face strict regulatory requirements that constrain how AI can be used. Understanding these requirements is essential for any organization deploying GenAI in these domains.

Healthcare: HIPAA and FDA

RegulationRequirementImpact on GenAI
HIPAA Privacy RuleProtect patient health information (PHI)PHI must never reach third-party LLM APIs without a BAA
HIPAA Security RuleTechnical safeguards for electronic PHIEncryption at rest and in transit, access controls, audit logs
FDA SaMDSoftware as a Medical Device regulationAI that diagnoses or recommends treatment may need FDA clearance
FDA 21 CFR Part 11Electronic records and signaturesAI-assisted clinical documentation needs audit trails

SR 11-7 (OCC/Fed)Model risk management for banksAI models used in lending decisions need full validation and documentation

Legal Industry Considerations

  • Attorney-client privilege: Sending client information to third-party AI services may waive privilege
  • Duty of competence: Lawyers must understand AI limitations (ABA Model Rule 1.1)
  • Court requirements: Several courts now require disclosure of AI use in legal filings
  • Hallucination risk: AI-generated legal citations must be verified (see Mata v. Avianca case)

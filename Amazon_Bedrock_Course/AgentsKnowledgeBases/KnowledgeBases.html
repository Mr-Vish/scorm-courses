Knowledge Bases and RAG

Bedrock Knowledge Bases

Knowledge Bases provide managed RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) that automatically ingests, chunks, embeds, and indexes your documents.

Supported Data Sources

  • Amazon S3: PDF, TXT, DOCX, HTML, CSV, MD files
  • Confluence: Wiki pages and spaces
  • SharePoint: Documents and sites
  • Salesforce: Knowledge articles
  • Web Crawler: Public web pages

Chunking Strategies

  • Fixed-size: Split documents into chunks of N tokens (default: 300)
  • Hierarchical: Parent-child chunks for better context retrieval
  • Semantic: Split on topic boundaries using embeddings
  • No chunking: Treat each file as one chunk (small documents only)

Introduction to Private LLM Gateways

As enterprises race to adopt Generative AI, they face a critical challenge: how to provide their employees and applications with access to powerful Large Language Models (LLMs) while maintaining strict security, privacy, and compliance standards. A Private LLM Gateway is the essential architectural component that solves this problem.

A Private LLM Gateway acts as a secure, centralized entry point for all AI requests within an organization. It sits between internal users/applications and external AI providers (like OpenAI, Anthropic, or Azure), providing a layer of control, visibility, and protection that is not possible with direct API access.

Why Your Organization Needs a Private Gateway

  • Data Protection: Automatically scan and redact Personally Identifiable Information (PII) before it ever leaves your internal network.
  • Access Control: Use your organization's existing Identity Provider (IDP) to manage who can access which models and at what cost.
  • Cost Management: Implement global and per-user budgets to prevent unexpected AI spend.
  • Compliance and Auditing: Maintain a complete, immutable log of every AI interaction for regulatory and forensic purposes.
  • Resilience: Implement automatic fallbacks and load balancing across multiple AI providers to ensure 24/7 availability.

Model Quantization: Optimizing LLMs for Efficiency

Welcome to Model Quantization: Optimizing LLMs for Efficiency. As Large Language Models grow in size and complexity, the ability to run them on standard hardware becomes a major challenge. Quantization is the essential technique that makes modern AI accessible, allowing us to shrink massive models by 70-80% with minimal loss in intelligence.

Architecture for AI Customer Support

System Components

  • Intent Classifier: Categorize incoming requests (billing, technical, general)
  • Knowledge Base: RAG over support docs, FAQs, product manuals
  • Conversation Manager: Maintain context across multi-turn conversations
  • Action Engine: Execute actions (create ticket, process refund, look up order)
  • Escalation System: Route to human agents when AI cannot resolve

Escalation Triggers

TriggerConditionAction
Low confidenceAI unsure about answer (<70% confidence)Offer to connect to human
Customer frustrationSentiment analysis detects angerPrioritize human handoff
Complex issueMultiple failed resolution attemptsAuto-escalate with context
Sensitive topicLegal, safety, or financial decisionsMandatory human review

Conversation Flow

  • 1. Greeting: Identify the customer (optional login)
  • 2. Intent detection: Classify the request category
  • 3. Information gathering: Ask clarifying questions if needed
  • 4. Resolution: Answer from knowledge base or execute action
  • 5. Confirmation: Verify the issue is resolved
  • 6. Feedback: Collect satisfaction rating

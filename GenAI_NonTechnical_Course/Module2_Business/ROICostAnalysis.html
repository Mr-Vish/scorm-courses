Cost Calculation Examples

Tool: ChatGPT Plus
Users: 10

Value Measurement Framework

Quantitative Metrics

  • Time Savings: Hours saved per week/month
  • Cost Reduction: Decreased spending on outsourcing, tools, labor
  • Revenue Impact: Increased sales, faster time-to-market
  • Productivity Gains: Output increase (content, code, analysis)
  • Quality Improvement: Error reduction, consistency gains

Qualitative Benefits

  • Employee Satisfaction: Reduced tedious work, focus on strategic tasks
  • Customer Experience: Faster responses, 24/7 availability
  • Innovation: More time for creative, strategic work
  • Competitive Advantage: Faster execution, better insights

Common ROI Pitfalls

Overestimating Adoption

Mistake: Assuming 100% of users will adopt immediately

Reality: Typical adoption curve: 20% month 1, 50% month 3, 80% month 6

Solution: Use conservative adoption rates in calculations

Underestimating Training Costs

Mistake: Assuming users will self-train

Reality: Effective training requires structured programs, ongoing support

Solution: Budget $500-2,000 per user for comprehensive training

Ignoring Opportunity Costs

Mistake: Not accounting for time spent on implementation

Reality: Teams spend 10-20% of time on AI adoption activities

Solution: Include opportunity costs in total cost calculation

Measuring Wrong Metrics

Mistake: Focusing only on time saved

Reality: Value comes from quality, innovation, strategic work enabled

Solution: Measure multiple value dimensions

Hidden Value Sources

Employee Retention

Employees using AI tools report 30% higher job satisfaction. Reducing turnover by 10% saves $50K-150K per prevented departure.

Faster Decision-Making

AI-enabled analysis reduces decision cycles from weeks to days. Value: faster market response, competitive advantage.

Knowledge Preservation

AI captures and codifies institutional knowledge. Value: reduced dependency on key individuals, faster onboarding.

Innovation Capacity

Freeing 20% of employee time enables innovation projects. Value: new products, process improvements, strategic initiatives.

Key Takeaways

  • Typical GenAI ROI ranges from 500% to 5,000%+ with 1-3 month payback
  • Direct costs are often small compared to value generated
  • Include training, integration, and governance in total cost
  • Measure multiple value dimensions, not just time savings
  • Use conservative assumptions for adoption and benefits
  • Hidden value (retention, innovation, knowledge) often exceeds direct savings

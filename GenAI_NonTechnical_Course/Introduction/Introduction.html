GenAI for Non-Technical Leaders and Decision Makers

Welcome to GenAI for Non-Technical Leaders and Decision Makers. This course demystifies Generative AI, providing business leaders, managers, and stakeholders with the knowledge to make informed decisions about AI adoption, strategy, and implementation.

Why This Course Matters

Generative AI is transforming every industry. Leaders who understand GenAI's capabilities, limitations, and business implications can drive competitive advantage, while those who don't risk falling behind. This course provides the essential knowledge without requiring technical expertise.

Real-World Impact: Companies implementing GenAI strategically report 20-40% productivity gains, 50-70% faster customer response times, and 3-5x content output. However, 60% of GenAI projects fail due to poor planning, unrealistic expectations, or inadequate governance.

Learning Objectives

By completing this course, you will be able to:

  • Explain GenAI concepts in plain language to stakeholders and teams
  • Identify high-value GenAI use cases for your organization
  • Evaluate GenAI vendors, models, and solutions objectively
  • Understand GenAI pricing models and calculate ROI
  • Assess risks including hallucinations, bias, privacy, and compliance
  • Develop GenAI adoption strategies aligned with business goals
  • Implement governance frameworks for responsible AI use
  • Manage change and drive employee adoption of GenAI tools
  • Make informed build vs. buy decisions for GenAI solutions

Course Structure

This course contains 3 comprehensive modules with 9 in-depth content pages, followed by assessments to validate your understanding.

Module 1: GenAI Fundamentals for Leaders

  • Understanding GenAI: What it is, how it works (simplified), key terminology
  • Capabilities and Limitations: What GenAI can and cannot do, common misconceptions
  • GenAI Landscape: Major models, vendors, deployment options

Module 2: Business Applications and ROI

  • Use Cases by Department: Marketing, sales, customer service, operations, HR, legal
  • ROI and Cost Analysis: Pricing models, cost calculation, value measurement
  • Vendor Evaluation: Selection criteria, RFP process, proof of concept

Module 3: Strategy and Governance

  • AI Strategy Development: Vision, roadmap, prioritization, resource allocation
  • Risk Management: Hallucinations, bias, privacy, security, compliance
  • Change Management: Adoption strategies, training, governance, measuring success

Pros and Cons Analysis

Comprehensive analysis of GenAI advantages (productivity, cost savings, innovation) and challenges (accuracy, costs, complexity, risks).

Assessments

**Target Audience:** Business executives, department heads, managers, product managers, strategy teams

## Course Structure

### Module 1: GenAI Fundamentals for Leaders
1. Understanding GenAI - What it is, how it works, key terminology in plain language
2. Capabilities and Limitations - What GenAI can/cannot do, misconceptions, realistic expectations
3. GenAI Landscape - Major models (GPT, Claude, Gemini, Llama), vendors, deployment options

### Module 2: Business Applications and ROI
4. Use Cases by Department - Marketing, sales, customer service, operations, HR, legal with metrics
5. ROI and Cost Analysis - Pricing models, cost calculation, value measurement, break-even analysis
6. Vendor Evaluation - Selection criteria, RFP process, POC guidelines, contract negotiation

### Module 3: Strategy and Governance
7. AI Strategy Development - Vision, roadmap, prioritization, resource allocation, success metrics
8. Risk Management - Hallucinations, bias, privacy, security, compliance, mitigation strategies
9. Change Management - Adoption strategies, training programs, governance, measuring success

### Assessments
- Module 1: 10 questions on fundamentals, capabilities, landscape
- Module 2: 10 questions on use cases, ROI, vendor evaluation
- Module 3: 10 questions on strategy, risk, change management
- Final: 25 questions covering all modules + pros/cons

## Content Highlights

### Module 1 Topics
- LLM, tokens, prompts, hallucinations, RAG, fine-tuning (plain language)
- Generative vs. predictive AI
- How GenAI learns (simplified)
- What GenAI can do well vs. cannot do reliably
- GPT-4, Claude 3, Gemini, Llama comparison
- Cloud vs. on-premise deployment
- API vs. UI access

### Module 2 Topics
- Department-specific use cases with ROI metrics
- Productivity gains: 20-40% engineering, 50-70% customer service, 3-5x marketing
- Pay-per-token, per-seat, per-query pricing
- Cost calculation examples
- Build vs. buy decision framework
- Vendor RFP template
- POC success criteria

### Module 3 Topics
- AI strategy canvas
- Prioritization matrix (value vs. effort)
- Risk register template
- Hallucination mitigation strategies
- Bias detection and mitigation
- GDPR, HIPAA, industry compliance
- Training program design
- Adoption metrics and KPIs
